About Us

The Heaven Cakes —
Made with Love & Care

The Heaven Cakes is your trusted neighbourhood cake shop in Perungudi, Chennai, serving the sweetest memories one slice at a time. We specialise in beautifully designed custom cakes for every occasion — from intimate birthdays to grand wedding celebrations.

Every cake we bake is made fresh, using quality ingredients and crafted with personal attention. Tell us your theme, your flavour, your vision — and we'll bring it to life, right here in Tirumalai Nagar, Perungudi.

  • Wide range of flavours — chocolate, vanilla, fruit & more
  • Photo cakes, fondant designs, tiered cakes
  • On-time delivery and pick-up from our shop
